What We Do

Safewell Cares provides specialized care to your loved ones, giving them all the help they they require to live healthy, better lives.

From supported accomodation within our facilities to assisted care within your own homes, adults needing social care as well as their loved ones can rest easy knowing Safewell Cares' wide range of personal care options has got them covered.

Our Services

Supported Accomodation

Within our facility, you're home away from home. Our trained caregivers only provide top notch health and life assistance in line with highest standards and practices obtainable.

Domiciliary Care

Our properly trained staff offer support services and personal care all over England, as circumstances demand .

Live-in Care

Receive care best suited to you or your loved ones right within the comfort of your home. Safewell Cares will provide all necessary health and support services you need without you leaving your home.

Adult Care

Adults aged 18+ with special needs can find a multitude of care and health options available to them within Safewell Cares.

Older adults aged 65 years or older can also benefit from personal care, medication aid, dementia care, chaperone and other services according to their specific wants.

Freelance Care

We also offer companionship, hospital-home care, domestic help and other ad-hoc care or special services depending on your circumstances.
